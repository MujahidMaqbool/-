Online Resources

With Wellyx, you can manage what your customers can view and purchase from the widget & customer app.

Landing Page - Widget & Customer App

With Wellyx, you can select a landing page, helping you direct your customers to a desired destination on the widget & customer app.

Home

Toggle on to display the home tab on your widget & app. Toggling off will result in customers being unable to view your featured items.

Memberships

Toggle on to display the memberships tab on the widget & app. Toggling off will result in customers being unable to purchase membership.

Memberships For Members

Toggle on to display the memberships tab for members on your widget and app. Toggling off will result in members being unable to purchase additional memberships.

Packages

Toggle on to display the packages tab on your widget & app. Toggling off will result in customers being unable to purchase packages.

Classes

Toggle on to display the classes tab on your widget & app. Toggling off will result in customers being unable to book/purchase classes.

Events

Toggle on to display the events tab on your widget & app. Toggling off will result in customers being unable to book/purchase events.

Services

Toggle on to display the services tab on your widget & app. Toggling off will result in customers being unable to book/purchase services.

Products

Toggle on to display the products tab on your widget & app. Toggling off will result in customers being unable to purchase products.
